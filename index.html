Lambda Chi Alpha's Annual Philanthropy Benefitting Feeding America

About

As our annual philanthropy event, we run a community food drive in support of our national philanthropy partner Feeding America. Throughout the week of events, we support this cause by raising food & monetary donations, as well as encouraging community engagement in helping to feed hungry families in the Phoenix area.

To celebrate the efforts of the community, the food drive initiative culminates with the main event: The Watermelon Bust. Last year, we were able to raise a total of 101,500 lbs. of food; we hope to double that number this year. If you are interested in helping our cause, you can donate, come to an event, or even consider being a sponsor.

Have any questions or want to get involved? Contact Jack Hanson or Ryan Theisen.
